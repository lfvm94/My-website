About ...

Hi, I'm Luis

I'm a Civil Engineer graduated from the University of Guanajuato (UG) in Guanajuato, Guanajuato, México. During those studies I participated in a couple of international academic programs at Northeastern Illinois University (NEIU) , USA as an undergraduate student - in 2015, and at Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden as a graduate student - in 2017, to enhance my capabilities exploring other scientific and engineering areas, places and ideas, by taking courses related to stochastic processes, stochastic optimization algorithms, The Finite Element Method and Structural design. In 2019 I obtained my Bachelor Degree in Civil Engineering with a Thesis work about optimization design of reinforcing steel for concrete columns through an original mathematical numerical approach developed at the Aula CIMNE-UG

Later, in 2020, my professional carrer and research continued with a Master's program in Science of Structures at the Autonomous University of Queretaro (UAQ), in which I could learn a lot more in the area of computaitonal mechanics, numerical methods, engineering software development and optimal/sustainable design of civil infrastructure projects and structures, mainly of reinforced concrete. In 2022 I obtained my Degree in Master of Science (Structures). It was during these studies when I realized that I wanted to do Research for the rest of my life, and from this point on my carrer was divided into two main paths: research and industry.

Research

Right after my Master's studies at the Autonomous University of Queretaro I went to the CIMAT (Centro de de Investigación en Matemáticas - Research Center in Mathematics) in Guanajuato city, Mexico, to continue my carrer path in the Graduate Department of Computational Science by learning more about Prorgramming, Algorithms, Parallel Computing, Numerical methods and Data Science. During these studies I could develop a research work related to optimization algorithms based on Combinatorial-Mathematics for the design of asymmetrical rebar in rectangular columns, which then led to additional research works related to optimization design of Irregular reinforced concrete

In 2023 I was accepted at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) for my PhD studies in Civil Engineering with focus in Scientific Computation. Here at the HKUST I focus in the area of Machine Learning, Structural Reliability and Numerical modelling for structural design and optimization.

Industry

Even though research is my priority when referring to my professional carrer I keep on involving myself in infrastructure projects in the private industry along with my father and my brother as a company. We perform structural designs, topographyc surveys, construction plans and budgets.

I usually come across with very interesting ideas by doing these sort of works. Thus, it is vital for me now to always be linked to the industrial sector somehow to enhance my research topics and scope.
